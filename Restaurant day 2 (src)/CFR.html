Chicken Fired Rice

Ingredients

1 large egg 1 tablespoon water 1 tablespoon butter 1 tablespoon vegetable oil 1 onion, chopped 2 cups cooked white rice, cold 1 cup cooked, chopped chicken meat 2 tablespoons soy sauce 1 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions

step 1. Gather all ingredients
step 2. Beat egg and water together in small bowl. Melt butter in a large skillet or wok over medium-low heat; add egg and cook without stirring, 1 to 2 minutes. Remove egg from skillet and cut into shreds; set aside.
step 3. Heat oil in the same skillet. Add onion; cook and stir until soft, about 3 minutes. Stir in rice, chicken, soy sauce, and pepper. Cook and stir for about 5 minutes; stir in shredded egg. Serve hot.
